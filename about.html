About Us

Ireland uses the most plastic packaging of any EU country. Plastic bottles were found at over 80% of all coastal sites surveyed during the All-Ireland Coastwatch Survey carried out in late 2017.

Our Water Stations promote and encourage people to drink more water without having to purchase bottled water. We want to empower society to break away from the over production of single-use plastic and its effect on our wellbeing and that of the environment.

Our Water Stations are smart and use cutting-edge technologies to relay data to a central hub and to your customised dashboard. You can display how much single-use plastic has been saved in real time. You can share this important information with your community which will encourage them to become part of the movement to reduce our dependence on single-use plastics. Dependence on single-use disposable plastic and the mass waste it creates, in the name of water as a commodity, directly affects our health and wellbeing, diminishes the health of our shores and affects ocean life.

For every Water Station we sell, Native Irish Broadleaf Trees are planted to offset our manufacturing emissions.
