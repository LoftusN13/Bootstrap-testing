Our Products

All design and manufacturing takes place in Ireland. Our Water Stations are made from heavy gauge marine-grade 316L stainless steel and are street furniture quality. We use 3D printing and the latest laser steel-cutting technology to make our products.

Each Water Station can be customised to suit your specific needs and design.

Option A: Standard Water Station

An affordable Water Station using unfiltered water direct from the mains.

This option has no smart technology on board.

Option B: Standard Filtered Water Station

An affordable Water Station using water direct from the mains that passes through our filter.

This option has no smart technology on board. There is a wall-mounted version of this Water Station.

Option C: Smart Water Station

A Smart Water Station using water direct from the mains that passes through our filter. This option includes access to our App and PC display screen plus a smart flat-pack TV for public display of the Water Station Dashboard and Information.

There is a wall-mounted version of this Water Station. This unit comes with a box that is installed on the other side of the wall to house services, filters and smart chip technology.

Option D: Solar Pack

A Smart Water Station that comes complete with:

  • Solar Panels
  • Stainless Steel Pole
  • Signage for End User
  • Battery Pack
  • Control Box
  • IP68 Rating
  • USB Charging Points

Option E: Earth Pack

A Smart Water Station that comes complete with:

  • The full Solar Pack
  • 1 x no. 100w Vertical Axis Turbine
  • 1 x LED Street Light

Option F: Natural Stone Plinths

Can be provided with your Water Station with or without your company/business logo.
